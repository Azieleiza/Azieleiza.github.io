Recipe #1: Cinnamon Biscuits

Ingredients Quantity
Cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon
Sugar 1/2 cup
Buttermilk Biscuits 10 ounces
Melted butter 1/4
Strawberry preserves 10 teaspoons

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and cinammon.
  2. Dip top and sides of biscuits in butter, then cinnamon.
  3. Place biscuits on ungreased baking sheets.
  4. make small indentations on biscuits, fill with strawberry preserve.
  5. Bake at 375°for 15 mins..

Recipe #2: Banana & Chocolate Puff pastry

Ingredients Quantity
Butter 2 tablespoons
Brown sugar 1/4 cup
Ripe bananas 2
frozen puff pastry 1 sheet
Semi sweet chocolate 4 ounces

Steps

  1. Melt butter in medium heat, stir with brown sugar.
  2. Add bananas, stir to coat.
  3. Unfold puff pastries, cut into 2 rectangles.
  4. Bake until golden brown, 20 mins.

Recipe #3: Raspberry Cheese Danish

Ingredients Quantity
Softened cream cheese 4 ounces
confectioners sugar 1 cup
refrigerated crescent rolls 1 can
White sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
seedless raspberry jam 1/2 cup
2% milk 2 teaspoons

Steps

  1. beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  2. unroll crescent rolls and separate into 4 rectangles. cut each in half.
  3. Spread cream diagonally across squares.
  4. top with 1 tablespoon of jam.
  5. Bring 2 opposite corners of squares together and pinch, sealing it.
  6. Bake at 375° until golden brown
